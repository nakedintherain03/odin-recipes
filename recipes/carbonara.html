Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta best known for the creaminess of it's sauce and the crunchiness of the meat

Ingredients

  • Guanciale
  • Egg
  • Pepper
  • Parmigiano
  • Salt
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Cut the guanciale into thick batons.
  2. Finely grate the parmigiana.
  3. Whisk together the egg. cheese and pepper in a large bowl.
  4. Cook the pasta in 4 litres of water with 1 tablespoon of salt.
  5. Scoop out a cup of pasta cooking water.Then drain the rest.
  6. Cook guanciale until it's golden.
  7. Toss the pasta in guanciale and toss it around so it gets coated in the fat.
  8. Transfer it all into the sauce bowl.
  9. Add a 1/2 cup of the cooking water into the bowl
  10. Mix!
  11. Finally, serve.
