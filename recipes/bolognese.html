Bolognese

Description

Bolognese is the most famous italian pasta recipe. Known for it's red sauce and fingerlicking taste.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Heavy cream
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Pasta of choice

Steps

  1. Fry the carrots, celery and onion.
  2. Stir in the beef.
  3. Pour in your jar of marinara sauce.
  4. Simmer over the course of 30 minutes, the sauce will thicken and cook down.
  5. When the sauce has reached your desired thickness, stir in the heavy cream.
  6. Boil some water for the pasta during the last 30 minutes of that simmer.
  7. Mix it all.
  8. Eat!
